Online Reading

Access digital books securely with your library card verification.

With your registered library card, you can access hundreds of digital books directly in your browser — no downloads needed. Simply browse the catalog, verify your card, and start reading instantly. Your reading progress is saved automatically, and you can return to any book at any time during your borrowing period.

Hard-Copy Borrowing

Visit the library in person to borrow physical books from our catalog.

Browse our full physical catalog online and reserve a book before you arrive. Present your library card at the desk to collect your reservation. Each member can borrow up to 3 books at a time for a 14-day period. Renewals can be requested online if no other member has reserved the same title.

Automated Fines

Transparent overdue fine tracking with configurable rates and waiver support.

Fines are calculated automatically based on the number of days a book is overdue. You can view your current fine balance anytime from your dashboard. Librarians can apply waivers for exceptional circumstances. All fine history is logged transparently so you always know what you owe and why.
